Improving the Lives of People with Disabilities

Creating economic opportunities for people with disabilities and ensuring their inclusion in the society

Welcome to REACT!

Based in Madurai, we are dedicated to creating economic opportunities for people with disabilities and ensuring their inclusion in the society that includes educational institutions, the workplace, and the community. Some of the key services to PwDs are career guidance, training, placement opportunities and self-employment opportunities.

We offer inclusion services to NGOs, government bodies, and parents across our region. Our services to employers include infrastructure audit, workplace policy changes, inclusion activities, sensitization workshops and manpower support.
