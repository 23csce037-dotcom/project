About Us

Resource Education And Charitable Trust (REACT) is a not-for-profit organization, registered under sections 80G and 12A, committed to uplifting economically weaker sections of society, with a focus on persons with disabilities and economically weaker sections (PwDs & EWS), LGBTQIA+, and Women. Our mission is to empower these vulnerable communities through knowledge, skills, and equal opportunities for sustainable economic growth.

REACT, established in 2023, by Ramesh Duraikannan, brings together his unique blend of factory and education industry experience. His career has been marked by a consistent dedication to bettering the lives of people in India and Africa. Notably, he has facilitated job placements for almost 1,500 individuals with disabilities in the last five years, underscoring his unwavering commitment to empowering marginalized communities.

Founder

Ramesh Duraikannan

Ramesh Duraikannan, an engineering graduate with over 20 years of experience in the education sector, founded REACT on 21 June 2023. His achievements include securing jobs for nearly 2,000 PwDs over the past 8 years, demonstrating his commitment to empowering vulnerable communities.

His vision is straightforward: to build an ecosystem where every space and service is accessible to everyone. REACT is working block by block, ensuring our support reaches people with disabilities across the community, making inclusion a reality one step at a time.

REACT partners with special schools and higher educational institutions to enhance their support systems, and we collaborate closely with NGOs and other community institutions to improve the lives of people with disabilities across Madurai.
